About EDYLIZ INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Edyliz Int'l School is located at No 18 Shedrack Avenue beside Rock Word Church Elelenwo, Port Harcourt. We run creche, Nursery, Primary, Secondary and after school sessions. We have well equipped , safe and conducive environment for learning, spacious classrooms, steady lights 24/7 with highly educated and qualified teachers with good moral principles. Edyliz school is the centre of excellence where children are given qualitative and quantitative education holistically. We run National and British curriculum.
